Seller Terms and Conditions
Quick Summary

As a Kids & Baby Store seller, you commit to: (1) Selling only safe, age-appropriate, certified products (2) Accurate descriptions and honest practices (3) Prompt fulfillment and excellent customer service (4) Compliance with child safety regulations (5) Ethical business conduct protecting children and families.

1. Acceptance & Purpose

By submitting a seller application and listing products on Kids & Baby Store ("Platform"), you agree to these Seller Terms and Conditions. These Terms form a binding agreement between you ("Seller") and the Platform. Our mission is to provide a safe, trusted marketplace for children's and baby products, prioritizing child safety above all else.

2. Seller Eligibility
  • Seller must be 18 years or older.
  • All information and documents submitted must be true, accurate, and current.
  • We reserve the right to approve, reject, suspend, or terminate seller access at our discretion.
3. Child Safety & Parental Protection (CRITICAL)

Special Obligations for Kids & Baby Products:

  • Safety First: All products must meet Philippine safety standards (DTI, FDA) and international standards (ASTM, EN71, CE where applicable)
  • Age-Appropriate: Products must include clear age recommendations and warnings (e.g., "3+", "Not for children under 36 months")
  • Zero Tolerance: Recalled items, expired food/formula, toxic materials (lead, BPA), counterfeit products, and hazardous items are strictly prohibited
  • Parental Trust: Parents rely on accurate information—misrepresentation of safety features is grounds for immediate ban
  • Documentation: Be prepared to provide safety certificates, test reports, or compliance documentation upon request
4. Product Compliance
  • Products must comply with applicable Philippine laws (Consumer Act RA 7394, Food Safety Act RA 10611), safety standards, and intellectual property rights.
  • Prohibited items include counterfeit goods, unsafe baby products, hazardous materials, restricted pharmaceuticals, recalled items, or any item violating regulations.
  • We may remove listings that are inaccurate, misleading, non-compliant, or pose safety risks without prior notice.
  • Special Requirements: Baby food/formula (FDA registration), toys (safety testing), cribs/car seats (certification), clothing (flame-retardant when required)
5. Listing Standards
  • Descriptions must be clear, truthful, and not misrepresent product quality, origin, or features.
  • Images must be high quality, unwatermarked (unless branding), and correspond to the actual product.
  • Variants (size, color, age group) must be structured properly to avoid buyer confusion.
6. Pricing & Promotions
  • You are responsible for setting fair and competitive prices.
  • Artificial price inflation followed by fake discounts is prohibited.
  • Platform may run site-wide promotions; participation terms will be communicated separately.
  • Family-Friendly Pricing: Avoid predatory pricing on essential baby items (diapers, formula, safety equipment)
7. Inventory & Fulfillment
  • Seller must maintain accurate stock levels. Overselling or frequent cancellations can lead to penalties.
  • Orders should be processed within 24–48 hours unless pre-order terms are disclosed.
  • Late fulfillment, unresponsive communication, or refusal to honor paid orders may affect seller status.
  • Priority for Parents: Understand that parents often need products urgently—honor timelines or communicate delays promptly
8. Shipping & Delivery
  • Seller must properly package items to prevent damage, especially for fragile baby products.
  • Use child-safe packaging materials (no sharp edges, excessive plastic wrap that could be a suffocation hazard)
  • Tracking information should be updated promptly where applicable.
  • Lost or damaged shipments due to inadequate packaging or negligence may be subject to dispute resolution and compensation.
9. Returns & Refunds
  • Sellers must honor the return policy declared in their store settings and comply with consumer protection laws.
  • Valid reasons for returns include defective, incorrect, or damaged items.
  • Disputes will be mediated; unresolved repeated issues may lead to suspension.
10. Seller Conduct
  • No harassment, abusive language, or fraudulent activity.
  • Directing buyers to transact off-platform to avoid fees is prohibited.
  • Spam messaging or manipulation of reviews will result in corrective action.
  • Family-Friendly Communication: Maintain professional, respectful tone—remember you're communicating with parents
11. Intellectual Property
  • You grant the Platform a non-exclusive, royalty-free license to use product images, descriptions, and branding for display and marketing.
  • Report suspected IP infringement via support channels; we act upon proper documentation.
  • No Counterfeits: Selling fake branded baby items is grounds for immediate permanent ban
12. Fees & Payouts
  • Platform fees and commission rates will be disclosed on your seller dashboard and updated terms.
  • Payouts are processed to your registered GCash account according to the payout schedule.
  • Disputed transactions may have funds held until resolution.
13. Account Suspension & Termination
  • Violations may result in warnings, temporary suspension, or permanent ban depending on severity.
  • Immediate Ban Triggers: Child safety violations, counterfeit products, fraud, harassment
  • Upon termination, pending orders must still be fulfilled or refunded.
14. Limitation of Liability
  • Platform acts as a marketplace facilitator; liability for product quality, safety, and delivery rests with the seller.
  • Seller agrees to indemnify Platform against claims arising from their products or conduct.
15. Amendments

We may update these Terms periodically. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of updated Terms. Significant changes will be communicated via email or dashboard notification.

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Child Safety Standards & Product Compliance
Critical Importance

As a seller on a kids & baby-focused platform, you have a heightened responsibility to ensure the safety, quality, and age-appropriateness of all products. Violations of these standards will result in immediate removal and may lead to legal consequences.

1. Age-Appropriate Products
  • Clearly label recommended age ranges (e.g., "0-6 months", "3+ years")
  • Products must comply with age-specific safety regulations
  • Small parts warning required for items not suitable for children under 3
  • Choking hazard warnings must be prominently displayed
2. Safety Certifications
  • All products must meet Philippine safety standards (DTI, FDA when applicable)
  • Toys must comply with ASTM F963 or equivalent international standards
  • Baby cribs, strollers, car seats must have certification proof
  • Food products require FDA registration and proper labeling
  • Clothing must use flame-retardant materials where required
3. Strictly Prohibited Items
  • Recalled products or items with known safety defects
  • Counterfeit toys or unlicensed character merchandise
  • Hazardous materials: Lead paint, toxic chemicals, BPA in baby bottles
  • Unsafe cribs/furniture with drop-side rails or improper spacing
  • Expired food, formula, or medications
  • Adult items or age-inappropriate content
  • Weapons or weapon-like toys for very young children
4. Accurate Labeling Requirements
  • Include age recommendations, warnings, and safety instructions
  • List all materials, especially if allergen-related (fabrics, fillings)
  • Provide assembly instructions for furniture or complex toys
  • Display nutritional information for baby food products
  • Include expiry dates for perishable or time-sensitive items
5. Quality & Condition Standards
  • Products must be new unless clearly marked as "pre-owned" or "gently used"
  • Used items must be thoroughly sanitized and free from damage
  • No products with missing parts, defects, or safety compromises
  • Packaging must protect products from damage during shipping
6. Accurate Product Descriptions
  • Never misrepresent product features, size, materials, or origin
  • Disclose all potential hazards or safety considerations
  • Provide clear images showing actual product condition
  • Include care instructions and maintenance requirements
7. Safe Shipping Practices
  • Use appropriate packaging to prevent damage (bubble wrap, sturdy boxes)
  • Secure small parts to prevent choking hazards during unboxing
  • Include safety documentation and assembly instructions
  • Label fragile items and handle with care
8. Customer Communication
  • Respond promptly to safety concerns or product questions
  • Provide clear instructions for assembly, use, and maintenance
  • Honor returns immediately for safety-related defects
  • Report safety incidents to platform administrators
9. Legal Compliance
  • Comply with Philippine Consumer Act (RA 7394)
  • Follow Department of Health (DOH) guidelines for health products
  • Adhere to Department of Trade and Industry (DTI) regulations
  • Respect intellectual property rights (no fake brands)
Best Practices for Sellers
  • Source products from reputable, certified manufacturers
  • Keep safety certificates and compliance documents on file
  • Stay updated on product recalls and safety alerts
  • Test products personally before listing
  • Provide excellent customer service for safety inquiries
  • Maintain proper storage to prevent product deterioration
  • Use child-safe packaging materials
  • Include thank-you notes with safety reminders
Consequences of Non-Compliance
  • First Violation: Warning and mandatory safety training
  • Second Violation: Temporary account suspension (7-30 days)
  • Serious Violation: Immediate permanent ban + withheld payouts
  • Legal Action: Platform may report violations to authorities (DTI, FDA)
  • Liability: Seller is solely responsible for injuries or damages caused by their products

Together, we protect the children and families who trust our platform.
