Kids Kingdom - Buyer Terms & Privacy

Kids Kingdom is an online marketplace for families. By creating a buyer account, you join a community focused on child safety, product quality, and service.

What Kids Kingdom Offers to Buyers

  • Curated products for newborns, toddlers, and children from trusted sellers.
  • Standardized product information and safety notes.
  • Secure payment options and delivery tracking.
  • Order support for delivery concerns.

Your Responsibilities as a Buyer

  • Provide accurate delivery details and contact information.
  • Review product descriptions and sizing guides before ordering.
  • Inspect items upon delivery and report issues within the return period.
  • Treat riders, support staff, and sellers with respect.

Data Privacy & Protection

  • Your data is used for account management, orders, and support only.
  • We do not sell personal information to third parties.
  • Limited data is shared only to complete deliveries.

Orders, Cancellations, and Returns

  • Prices are in PHP and may change based on promotions or seller updates.
  • Orders may be cancelled only within the allowed window.
  • Approved returns and refunds follow the Return Policy timelines.

Acceptance of Buyer Terms
