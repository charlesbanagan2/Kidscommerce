Welcome to Kids Kingdom – Buyer Terms & Privacy

Kids Kingdom is a dedicated online marketplace for parents and guardians who want safe, high-quality products for babies and kids. By creating a buyer account, you are joining a community focused on child safety, product quality, and excellent service.

What Kids Kingdom Offers to Buyers

  • Curated products for newborns, toddlers, and children from trusted and vetted sellers.
  • Standardized product information (age-appropriateness, safety notes, and usage guidelines).
  • Secure payment options (GCash, cards, and COD where available).
  • Order tracking and direct communication with riders and support for delivery concerns.

Your Responsibilities as a Buyer

  • Provide accurate, up-to-date delivery details and contact information.
  • Review product descriptions and sizing guides carefully before placing orders.
  • Inspect items upon delivery and report issues within the allowed return period.
  • Treat riders, support staff, and sellers with courtesy and respect.

Data Privacy & Protection

  • Kids Kingdom uses your personal data solely for account management, order fulfillment, and customer support.
  • We do not sell your personal information to advertising networks or unrelated third parties.
  • Limited data (such as name, contact number, and delivery address) is shared only with riders and logistics partners to complete your delivery.
  • We implement reasonable security safeguards to protect your data from unauthorized access.

Orders, Cancellations, and Returns

  • Prices are shown in Philippine Peso (PHP) and may change based on promotions or seller updates.
  • Orders may be cancelled only within the time window indicated in the checkout or order page.
  • Approved returns and refunds are processed within the timelines disclosed in our Return Policy.
  • Repeated misuse of cancellation, COD refusal, or fraudulent behavior may result in account review or suspension.

Acceptance of Buyer Terms

  • You have read and understood the Kids Kingdom buyer terms, privacy practices, and policies.
  • You consent to the collection and processing of your data for the purposes described above.

Welcome to Kids Kingdom – Rider Terms & Privacy

Kids Kingdom is a trusted online marketplace for safe, high-quality products for babies and kids. As a rider, you play a crucial role in ensuring timely and careful delivery of orders to our customers.

What Kids Kingdom Expects from Riders

  • Deliver orders promptly and handle packages with care.
  • Maintain professional conduct with customers and support staff.
  • Keep your vehicle in good working condition and follow traffic regulations.
  • Protect customer data and privacy at all times.

Your Benefits as a Rider

  • Flexible working hours and delivery schedules.
  • Competitive compensation per delivery.
  • Be part of a trusted community serving families.
  • Access to support and training resources.

Privacy and Data Protection

  • We only collect necessary information for delivery operations.
  • Your personal data is encrypted and stored securely.
  • We never share your data with third parties without consent.
  • You can request data deletion at any time.

Important Reminders

  • Always verify order details before pickup.
  • Report any issues immediately to support.
  • Maintain professional appearance and behavior.
  • Follow all local traffic and delivery regulations.
